Who Are We?

Ghost Kitchens is a groundbreaking and innovative company connecting talented, local chefs with local hungry customers via a take-out and delivery app and website. Opening a restaurant isn’t for everyone ... between space rental, decor, dishes, equipment, and staff to name a few things, the cost of opening a new restaurant can easily be hundreds of thousands of dollars. Our mission at Ghost Kitchens is for you to forget about all of those costs and focus on your passion and what you do best; create.

group

What do we do?

In a nutshell, Ghost Kitchens handles all of the marketing, advertising, website, guest checkout and many other costs associated with running a restaurant, and all of this for next to nothing. We are simply ... a bridge that connects you to customers. We’re designing our website to be extremely user-friendly and straightforward. Each chef will have their own admin page to add their restaurant name, bio, menu, and much more.

What am I responsible for?

The food side of things is quite literally your business. You choose how many days a week you want to cook and what you want to cook. Think of it as your restaurant, ...but in the comfort of your home. You build your menu, provide a description and picture for each dish and set pricing.
